Jerk Chicken

This is a recipe for Jerk Chicken.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Spring Onions
  • All Spice
  • Chilli Powder
  • Ginger
  • Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Dark Brown Sugar
  1. Add all ingreds to a blender and blend until combined but not smooth
  2. Coat chicken thighs in marinade
  3. Cook for 30 mins at 160 and 20 mins at 200
